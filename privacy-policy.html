Privacy Policy

Last updated: 18 July 2026

What we collect

When you submit our enquiry form, we collect: your name, phone number, email address, city/state, preferred destination and university, NEET score, 12th percentage, and any message you write. We don't collect anything beyond what you enter in the form.

How we use it

Solely to contact you about MBBS-abroad admissions guidance — a counsellor call, email, or WhatsApp message. We don't sell, rent, or share your information with third parties for marketing or advertising purposes.

Where it's stored

Enquiry form submissions are stored in a private Google Sheet accessible only to the Mathura Overseas team. We don't use advertising trackers or sell data to data brokers. If you message us on WhatsApp, that conversation is subject to WhatsApp's own privacy policy.

Cookies & local storage

This site uses local browser storage only for basic functionality (like remembering you've seen a popup) — not for advertising or cross-site tracking. No third-party ad or analytics cookies are set.

Your rights

You can ask us to access, correct, or delete your information at any time by contacting us below.
