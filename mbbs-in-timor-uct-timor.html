About this university

Dili's first Catholic university, with its MBBS program built directly on the Indian medical curriculum — taught fully in English by faculty from India, the USA, Cuba and Timor-Leste. A newer, lower-competition option for students who want an Indian-style syllabus without NEET-level seat pressure.

Why students choose it

  • Built on the Indian medical curriculum
  • Faculty from India, USA, Cuba & Timor-Leste
  • Total cost ~$35,000–40,000 (₹29–33L)
  • Tuition waivers available for top performers
