Why go abroad

What families gain by studying MBBS abroad

Transparent, Lower Fees

Total course costs are a fraction of Indian private-college donations — with every fee disclosed upfront.

Global Recognition

NMC, WHO and ECFMG-recognised degrees, valid for practice in India and abroad after screening.

NEET-Based, No Donation

A qualifying NEET score is all that's required — no capitation or seat-donation payments.

English Medium Throughout

Every partner program we work with teaches entirely in English — no language transition required.

Real Clinical Exposure

Affiliated teaching hospitals with thousands of combined beds give hands-on training from early years.

A Genuinely Global Career

Pathways open toward FMGE/NExT in India, or USMLE for the US, depending on where you study.

Before you apply

Eligibility criteria

Academic Qualification

Passed 10+2 with Physics, Chemistry, Biology.

Minimum Marks

50% aggregate in PCB (40% for SC/ST/OBC).

Age Requirement

17 years completed by December 31st.

NEET Qualification

Mandatory for all Indian applicants.

Valid Passport

Minimum 18 months' validity at the time of travel.

English Proficiency

Basic communication skills — no IELTS/TOEFL required for most partners.
