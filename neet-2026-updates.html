Tips & Guidance
Marks vs Rank, Explained

In 2025, 502 marks was worth roughly AIR 50,000. This year, it takes 535 marks to reach that same rank. If you're mentally comparing your 2026 score against a "safe score" you heard about from last year, you're likely working with the wrong number — the gap compounds further down the rank list too.

NEET News
Why This Year Is Different

The original NEET UG 2026 exam (May 3) was cancelled following paper leak allegations. NTA conducted a fresh Re-NEET on June 21, 2026, with 22+ lakh candidates appearing. A different exam means a different difficulty level and a different score distribution — which is exactly why comparing your marks to last year's cutoffs won't give you an accurate picture this time.

Official Advisory
Good News: Philippine MD Degrees Confirmed NMC-Compliant

The Embassy of India in Manila has confirmed that the 54-month MD programme at CHED-recognised Philippine institutions meets India's Foreign Medical Graduate Licentiate requirements — covering the prescribed subjects, English-medium instruction, a 12-month internship, and a Certificate of Registrability. The Philippine House of Representatives separately endorsed the same compliance in March 2026. One nuance: practicing within the Philippines itself still needs the local licensure exam, pending reciprocity confirmation between the two countries — this doesn't affect graduates planning to practice in India or several other countries. Always confirm NMC compliance for your specific university before enrolling. Read the full advisory.

3
Round 2 & Round 3

Stray Vacancy Round

Final round for leftover seats, expected around October 2026. Carries the strictest reporting deadlines of the whole cycle.
