About studying MBBS in Vietnam

Vietnam has quickly become one of the closest and most affordable MBBS-abroad options for Indian students — around a 4-hour flight from India, with NMC and WHO-recognised universities including Hanoi Medical University. Programs run 6 years (5 years of study plus a 1-year internship), taught in English, with tuition significantly lower than private medical colleges in India and no donation or capitation fees.

Why students choose it

  • NMC, WHO and FAIMER-recognised universities
  • Among the closest MBBS-abroad destinations to India
  • English-medium instruction throughout
  • Indian food and hostel facilities on most campuses
  • No donation or capitation fees
